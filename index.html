About the Photographer

Mohana Krishnan

Hello, I'm Mohana Krishnan, an aspiring nature photographer from India. Photography is a journey I'm currently exploring, and it has quickly become one of my favorite ways to connect with the world around me. Through my camera, I enjoy capturing the small details and moments in nature that often go unnoticed.

I'm particularly interested in photographing nature, landscapes, and everyday scenes from my travels and walks. As a beginner, I'm constantly learning about composition, lighting, and storytelling through images. Every photograph is an opportunity to improve, experiment, and share my perspective of the natural world.
